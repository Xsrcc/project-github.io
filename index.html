About me

. "Fotografi hebat adalah tentang kedalaman perasaan, bukan kedalaman bidang." XsrCC,

Our photography experience stretches back for more than 15 years – with Pauline’s family heritage consisting of two generations of photographers! Pauline has her roots in fashion photography – a style which has always influenced our work – and together, we have worked as a wedding photographer and filmmaker for more than a decade. It’s incredibly important for us to blend with the wedding itself and work in a subtle and unobtrusive way. We work with the flow of the day, reflecting the emotions of the wedding party and guests, and highlighting the look and feel of our surroundings..
